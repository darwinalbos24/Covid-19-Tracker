Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a college degree in web designing/developing? No I don't. I am a college undergraduate from Adamson University with the course of B.S. Architecture. I had to halt my education for some personal reasons, which is why I took up other side courses that can aid me in having additional skills.

Why did you create this website? The original concept for this is to display the current Covid-19 data cases we have in the Philippines, but I figured I can add additional information so that it will become a one stop site that contains all information about this virus. Moreover, this is the platform that I know how to utilize, in order to help inform others.

Where did you get your certificate about Covid-19? I got my certificate from TESDA's course about preventive measures of Covid-19 in the workplace. We were required to study and listen about 2.5 hours worth of lecture about Covid-19. There is an assessment at the end where I got 91% passing rate (32/35 questions). The lecture covered basically everything we need to know about this virus including its history, common and unusual symptoms, proper methods of protecting yourself, etc.

How much did you spend for your TESDA courses? I finished all my courses without spending a single penny. Alongside this, I also received allowance during the course duration which makes everything worthwhile.

How much do you earn in this website? I do not earn any amount of money from this website. I acquired all the data I used here for free, which is why I do not wish for any kind of advertisement nor solicitations. This is purely for the benefit of public's knowledge about Covid-19. If you wish to donate, I suggest to look for charities and other orgnizations that really focuses on our fight against Covid-19.
